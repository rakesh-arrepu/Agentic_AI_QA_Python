Requirement: The e-commerce platform shall allow users to add, view, and remove items in a shopping cart.

Details:
- Users can add products to the cart from the product listing or product detail page.
- The cart should display the product name, quantity, and price.
- Users can update the quantity of each product.
- Removing a product should update the total price immediately.
- The cart should persist if the user logs out and logs back in.
- A “Proceed to Checkout” button should be available when the cart has at least one item.
